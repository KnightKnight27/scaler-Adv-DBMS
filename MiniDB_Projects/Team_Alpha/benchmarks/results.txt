=================  MiniDB BENCHMARKS  =================

## Benchmark 1: Index Scan vs Sequential Scan (point lookup)
rows       seqScan(us/query)    idxScan(us/query)    speedup   
2000       1972                 340                  5.8x      
10000      2651                 94                   28.2x     
40000      16632                69                   241.0x    
Index point lookup is ~constant (O(log n)); seq scan grows linearly with rows.

## Benchmark 2: Buffer Pool Hit Rate
Read-only scan phase: hits=2050 misses=0 hitRate=100.0%
Hot pages stay cached, so repeated scans hit the buffer pool instead of disk.

## Benchmark 3: MVCC vs 2PL Reader Blocking Under Write Contention
(1 writer with ~50us lock hold + 1 reader, single hot row)
scheme       avg read lock-acquire wait (us)
2PL          32.50                         
MVCC         6.16                          
Reader wait reduction with MVCC: 5.3x less waiting per read
2PL readers block until the writer releases the exclusive lock on the hot row;
MVCC readers see a consistent snapshot immediately and never wait.
